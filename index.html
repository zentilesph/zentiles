zentiles faqs

1. How big are the tiles?

Available sizes include:

  • 8″ × 8″ (21.35 × 21.35 cm)
  • 8″ × 11″ or 11″ × 8″ (21.35 × 27.94 cm or vice versa)
  • 12″ × 12″ (31.6 × 31.6 cm)
  • 12″ × 16″ or 16″ × 12″ (31.6 × 41.75 cm or vice versa)
  • 20″ × 20″ (49.53 × 49.53 cm)
  • 20″ × 27″ or 27″ × 20″ (49.53 × 68.58 cm or vice versa)

Note: Larger sizes available in other regions are not offered in the Philippines.

2. How long does shipping take?

Expect delivery within 10 days of ordering. Shipping is available only within the Philippines.

  • Standard shipping: ₱0 for orders above a certain threshold, otherwise a small fee applies.
  • Express shipping: Region-dependent, option available during checkout.
3. Can I mix sizes and styles in one order?

Yes! You can mix various sizes and styles in a single order.

4. Can the tiles be reused and removed cleanly?

Absolutely! The adhesive is formulated to stick securely while removing cleanly, leaving no wall damage. The tiles are reusable and maintain their structure after multiple repositionings.

5. What if I'm unhappy with my order?

Happiness Guarantee:

  • No returns for personalized products.
  • If your order is damaged, defective, or lost in transit, we’ll send a replacement at no charge.
6. Where is this produced?

Tiles are produced in the USA, UK, and Germany. Zentiles operates online only and has no physical stores in the Philippines.

7. How much do the tiles weigh?

Example weights for framed tiles:

  • 8″ × 8″: ~125 g
  • 12″ × 16″: ~304 g
  • 20″ × 20″: ~475 g
